Electricity


1. Beginning in the late 18th century the industrial revolution began changing the world
2. In 1819 a Dane, Hans Christian Oersted discovered that electric current in a wire
caused a nearby compass needle to move.
3. The Englishman Michael Faraday (1791-1867) invented the dynamo
4. In 1837 Samuel Morse invented the electric telegraph
5. In 1876 Alexander Graham Bell invented the telephone.
6. In 1878 Joseph Swan invented the light bulb.
7. But the dawn of the “Age of electricity came when Thomas Edison created a new system of electric light,
in 1882 for New York City, called, the “electric light bulb”.
8. Companies that let to the production of said machinery such as General Electric,
and Westington became the first multinational companies in the world.
9. These inventions made life more simple for large amounts of people.
